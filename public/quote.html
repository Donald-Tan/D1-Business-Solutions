Common questions

Before you reach out

Quick answers to what most clients ask us first.

Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?

No. We work on a 50% deposit to start, 50% on final approval before launch.

How long does a typical project take?

Starter sites are usually live within 5–7 business days. Growth and Scale packages typically take 2–4 weeks depending on content and integrations. We deliver a first draft in 48 hours.

What if I already have a website?

No problem — we can redesign it, optimize it, or add AI features on top of what you have. We'll audit your current site on the discovery call and recommend the fastest path forward.

Do I own my website after it's built?

100% yes. You own all the code, the content, and the domain. We never hold your site hostage — you can take it to any host or developer at any time, no questions asked.

What does the AI chatbot actually do?

It's trained on your business — your services, pricing, FAQs, and tone. It can answer questions, collect leads, qualify prospects, and book appointments, 24/7, without you lifting a finger.

Can I start with Starter and upgrade later?

Absolutely. We build everything modularly so adding AI, e-commerce, or automations later is straightforward. Just email us and we'll send a scoped add-on quote.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Just your logo (or we can help create one), some photos or we'll source them, and the core info about your business. We'll guide you through everything else on the discovery call.

Do you work with businesses outside Seattle?

Yes — we work remotely with clients across the US. Everything is handled over calls, email, and shared docs. Location is never a barrier.
